DEATH

Moose Wala was shot dead by unidentified assailants in his car on 29 May 2022 in Jawaharke village of Mansa. According to police, Lawrence Bishnoi's gang initially claimed responsibility for the murder in an unverified Facebook post, which Bishnoi denied making,and he was being held by the Punjab Police as of June 2022 and was considered the "mastermind" of the murder by officials.
